ASSESSMENT OF ECOLOGICAL PARAMETERS AND MORPHOLOGICAL CHARACTERISTICS of Goniopsis cruentata (Latreille, 1803) and Afruca tangeri (Eydoux, 1835) IN MANGROVE VEGETATION IN LAGOS STATE, NIGERIA.

Objectives of the study:
To assess the relative abundance of Goniopsis cruentata and Afuca tangeri in the mangrove area.
To determine the spatial and temporal distribution of G. cruentata and A. tangeri in the mangrove area.
To examine the morphometric characteristics and length-weight relationship of G.cruentata and A. tangeri in the mangrove area.
Determine the sex ratio of G. cruentata and A. tangeri in the mangrove area and the sediment quality of the wetlands in the mangrove area.

Researchers: Nicholas Eteobong (Msc), Oluwatubosun Ajayi (Msc), Odulate D.O. (PhD) and Faith Oyewale OLABISI
